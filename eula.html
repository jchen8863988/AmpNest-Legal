5. Charging Spot Booking and Queue System

5.1 Booking Rules

Use of the charging spot booking and queue features is subject to the rules displayed within AmpNest. Key rules include:

  • App users have booking priority over web (QR code) users
  • Pro+ users may book up to 48 hours in advance; Pro users up to 24 hours; Free users for the current day only
  • Users who do not arrive within the applicable grace period (15 minutes for Pro+, 10 minutes for Free/Pro) forfeit their spot to the next person in queue
  • Cancellations within 30 minutes of start time that exceed 3 per calendar month may result in temporary reduction of queue priority

5.2 No Guarantee of Spot Availability

AmpNest facilitates coordination between neighbors using shared charging infrastructure. The Developer does not own, operate, or control any charging equipment. We make no guarantee that a charging spot will be available at any particular time, that bookings will be honored by other users, or that the physical equipment will be functional. Any disputes between neighbors regarding charging spot use are solely between the parties involved.
